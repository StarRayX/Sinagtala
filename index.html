Bring into Light Unforeseen Opportunities

With this initiative, the SSG will provide information and guidance in partnership with the school about in school, national, and international competitions and events where every TPSians can inquire and participate. There are lots of activities that could have increased our school’s profile in different disciplines; However, there is a lack of awareness about the presence of these opportunities.


Bright Clubs and Ignited Organizations

Clubs and organizations are great extracurricular activities. Despite that fact, clubs and organizations in school are mostly low-impact (with just one being high-impact). There is a lack of exposure when it comes to what our clubs and organizations do and have accomplished to people outside school. This initiative aims to increase publicity to what our clubs and organizations can do through partnerships with different organizations and increased participation of student members. With these, our low-impact clubs and organizations can turn into high impact ones.


Star Buddy Program

This initiative strives to stimulate students’ critical thinking abilities through small group discussions (SGDs). Through this, students who normally won’t participate in class will be encouraged to share their ideas, perspectives, and confusions, thus promoting active learning. This will be effective if implemented every after class ends. This initiative also includes mentorship programs and student-to-student tuitions where student volunteer mentors guide students explore their shared interests while balancing a good academic standing.

Light the Way for Safety

Installment of waiting shed in the bus stop and finding a safer pathway where students can walk to go to the gas station and the nearby metro. This will be a collaboration between the student government and the parents’ organization in school.


Counselors, Gleaming Backbones for Wellbeing

With this initiative, we will work closely with the Guidance Department to raise awareness about mental health issues and create support systems for students facing challenges while also giving parents guidance of what their children may have been experiencing through workshops and conferences. This initiative will help parents know the best measure to take in order to help their children cope with their struggles in school.

Directing Light to Comfortability

With this initiative, sanitary items will be offered to comfort rooms.


Directing Light to Accessibility

With this initiative, printers will be accessible to students coming from second and third floor where there is a designated place each floor where students can print. Adding more printers is part of this initiative too.

A Brighter, Greener Tomorrow!

With this initiative, recycling programs will be brought back such as the collection and crashing of plastic water bottles. This also focuses on promoting responsible waste management, and advocates for eco-friendly practices, especially during school events. At the same time, this aims to encourage students to lessen their use of plastic materials that can only be used for once.


The Fountain for Healthier Environment

Installation of water refill stations in every floor. With this, there will be a decrease in purchase and use of plastic water bottles in school, thus also promoting the use of tumblers that are far more sustainable.

Honesty Spotlighted

Expansion of Honesty Store. Through this, we can offer more products to the student body as well as gaining consensus as to what is collectively needed.

You are A Star!

This initiative aims to recognize each student's effort in achieving excellence. This shares a similar concept to awarding the Model Student; However, this initiative branches out to the different subjects. If students are recognized for their good work, they will be encouraged and motivated to continue and push the walls of settling for the average.
